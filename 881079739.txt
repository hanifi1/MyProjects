MONTREAL, Jan. 21, 2019 /CNW Telbec/ - Calin Rovinescu, Air Canada President and Chief Executive Officer, Michael Rousseau, Deputy Chief Executive Officer &amp; Chief Financial Officer, and Lucie Guillemette, Executive Vice President &amp; Chief Commercial Officer, will hold a conference call for analysts on Friday, February 15, 2019 to present fourth quarter and full year 2018 results and be available for analysts' questions. Following the analysts' questions, Mr. Rousseau and Pierre Houle, Managing Director and Treasurer, will be available to answer questions from term loan B lenders and holders of Air Canada bonds.
