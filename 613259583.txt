TORONTO, Feb. 7, 2017 /CNW Telbec/ - Air Canada invites members of the media to meet talented Canadians Tyler Brûlé of Winkreative, fashion designer Christopher Bates, chef David Hawksworth and sommelier Véronique Rivest, in Toronto on February 9 and learn how they are helping to shape the future of Air Canada. The four will join Benjamin Smith, President, Passenger Airlines, at a special unveiling event followed by photo opportunities. 
Parking for media will be available on site. For accredited media travelling from downtown Toronto, a complimentary shuttle will depart from 55 Bremner Blvd. at 11 a.m. (media are asked to arrive at 10:30 a.m.) and departs from the Air Canada hangar at 1 p.m. to return downtown. Media are asked to RSVP by email to [email protected] to confirm their attendance.
