MONTREAL, Jan. 24, 2019 /CNW Telbec/ - Chris Isford, Vice President and Controller, and Kathleen Murphy, Director, Investor Relations and Corporate Reporting, will host a conference call on February 21, 2019 to discuss Air Canada's accounting policy changes under IFRS 16 Leases and the related accounting impacts as well as answer questions from analysts about this accounting standard change. 
On January 1st, 2019, Air Canada adopted the International Financial Reporting Standard 16 – Leases (IFRS 16 – Leases).  Air Canada's 2018 Audited Consolidated Financial Statements and Notes and its 2018 Management's Discussion and Analysis of Results of Operations and Financial Condition, which will be filed on SEDAR at www.sedar.com and posted on Air Canada's website at aircanada.com on February 15, 2019, will include a discussion of the changes expected under this new accounting standard.
