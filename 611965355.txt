MONTREAL, Jan. 27, 2017 /CNW Telbec/ - Air Canada will host its 2017 Investor Day on September 19, 2017, in Toronto from 8:00 a.m. to noon ET. Presenters include Calin Rovinescu, President and Chief Executive Officer, Benjamin Smith, President, Passenger Airlines, Klaus Goersch, Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer, and Michael Rousseau, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, with other members of Air Canada's executive team present.
