MONTREAL, Jan. 25, 2018 /CNW Telbec/ - Air Canada recently launched aircanada.com/media, a media-rich news hub and resource to help journalists and digital content creators source the latest in Air Canada news, travel news and aviation news. The Media Centre includes Air Canada's press releases, featured stories for editorial use, high-res photos and downloadable videos, background information, company statements, as well as helpful industry links.
