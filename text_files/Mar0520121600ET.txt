 MONTREAL, March 5, 2012 /CNW Telbec/ - For the month of February, Air Canada reported a system load factor of 76.8 per cent, versus 75.9 per cent in February 2011, an increase of 0.9 percentage points.  System traffic increased 6.5 per cent on a system-wide capacity increase of 5.3 per cent. Air Canada reports traffic results on a system-wide basis, including regional airlines from which Air Canada purchases capacity.
