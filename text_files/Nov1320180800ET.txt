MONTREAL, Nov. 13, 2018 /CNW Telbec/ - Air Canada will host its 2019 Investor Day on February 28, 2019, in Toronto from 9:00 a.m. to Noon ET. Presenters will include Calin Rovinescu, President and Chief Executive Officer, Michael Rousseau, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, and Lucie Guillemette, Executive Vice President and Chief Commercial Officer. The presenters will be joined by other members of the executive team.
Attendance is limited to investors and analysts. Media and the public may access this webcast on a listen-in basis.
