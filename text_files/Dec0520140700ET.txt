Awarded 'Best Airline in North America' for 10th time  
MONTREAL, Dec. 5, 2014 /CNW Telbec/ - Air Canada has won multiple awards in reader surveys conducted by the world's leading travel magazines, including being named for the tenth consecutive year "Best Airline in North America" by Global Traveler magazine and for the seventh consecutive year "Best North American Airline for International Travel" by Business Traveler magazine.
