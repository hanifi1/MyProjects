 MONTREAL, May 9, 2013 /CNW Telbec/ - Air Canada will host its 2013 Investor Day in Toronto on June 10, 2013.  The day will begin at 08:30 with opening remarks by Calin Rovinescu, President and Chief Executive Officer, followed by presentations by Ben Smith, Executive Vice President and Chief Commercial Officer, Klaus Goersch, Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer and Michael Rousseau, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer.  The presentations are scheduled to end at 12:30.
