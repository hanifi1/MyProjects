 MONTREAL, Dec. 17, 2012 /CNW Telbec/ - Media are invited to attend a news conference where Air Canada senior executives will present the company's new leisure carrier. Ben Smith, Executive Vice President and Chief Commercial Officer, Air Canada and Michael Friisdahl, President and Chief Executive Officer of Air Canada's Leisure Group will discuss details and strategy of Air Canada's latest venture, followed by a question period and interview availability.
