 TORONTO, Feb. 27, 2012 /CNW Telbec/ - Air Canada invites media to attend an open house celebration of its 75th anniversary on Friday, March 2, at Lester B. Pearson International Airport. Media will have an opportunity to tour Boeing's 787 Dreamliner, which is making a first visit to Canada for the event, and speak with Air Canada and Boeing officials about the world's newest and most modern commercial aircraft. Air Canada has committed to purchase 37 Dreamliners with first delivery in 2014.
