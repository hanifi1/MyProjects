TORONTO, Nov. 18, 2015 /CNW Telbec/ - Air Canada invites accredited media to the City of Toronto's Signature Event with Mayor John Tory on Thursday, November 19. Media will have an opportunity to board Air Canada's new 298-seat Boeing 787-9 Dreamliner and explore the latest in travel amenities, including next generation lie-flat suites in International Business Class and the new Premium Economy cabin. Air Canada has firm orders for 37 Dreamliners.
What:  
Air Canada is co-hosting an evening with Mayor John Tory to recognize Toronto businesses and celebrate Toronto's role as a global city.
When: 
Thursday, November 19, 2015
