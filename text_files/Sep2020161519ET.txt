TORONTO, Sept. 20, 2016 /CNW Telbec/ - Air Canada invites the media to attend a groundbreaking ceremony to mark the start of construction of the airline's new Hangar 5 at Toronto Pearson airport. Once complete, the new hangar will cover an area equal to seven professional hockey rinks in a state-of-the-art facility capable of accommodating five aircraft at one time.
