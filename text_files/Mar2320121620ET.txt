 MONTREAL, March 23, 2012 /CNW Telbec/ - Although Air Canada ground handling employees at Toronto and Montreal international airports have returned to work following an illegal work stoppage this morning, delays and cancellations of Air Canada-operated flights primarily to Canadian and U.S. destinations are expected for the remainder of the day. 
 Customers are requested to check the status of their flight at aircanada.com before leaving for the airport.
