MONTREAL, April 23, 2019 /CNW Telbec/ - Calin Rovinescu, Air Canada President and Chief Executive Officer, Michael Rousseau, Deputy Chief Executive Office and Chief Financial Officer, and Lucie Guillemette, Executive Vice President and Chief Commercial Officer, will hold a conference call for analysts on Monday, May 6, 2019 to present first quarter results and be available for analysts' questions. Following the analysts' questions, Mr. Rousseau and Pierre Houle, Managing Director and Treasurer, will be available to answer questions from term loan B lenders and holders of Air Canada bonds. 
Immediately following the first quarter earnings call, Chris Isford, Vice President and Controller and Kathleen Murphy, Director, Investor Relations and Corporate Reporting will host a discussion on the accounting policies related to Air Canada's acquisition of the Aeroplan loyalty program and will be available for analysts' questions related thereto.
