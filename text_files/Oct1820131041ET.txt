 MONTREAL, Oct. 18, 2013 /CNW Telbec/ - Air Canada will hold a conference call for analysts on Friday, November 8, 2013 to present 2013 third quarter results.  Air Canada President and Chief Executive Officer, Calin Rovinescu, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, Michael Rousseau, and Executive Vice President and Chief Commercial Officer, Ben Smith will be available for analysts' questions. Immediately following the analysts' Q&amp;A session, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, Michael Rousseau, and Treasurer, Pierre Houle, will be available to answer questions from bond holders.
