 MONTREAL, Feb. 5, 2014 /CNW Telbec/ - For the month of January, Air Canada reported a record system load factor of 80.0 per cent, versus 79.4 per cent in January 2013, an increase of 0.6 percentage points on a system-wide capacity increase of 3.0 per cent.  On this additional capacity, system traffic for January increased 3.7 per cent.  Air Canada reports traffic results on a system-wide basis, including Air Canada rougeTM, which began operations on July 1, 2013, and regional airlines from which Air Canada purchases capacity.
